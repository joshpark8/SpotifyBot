**$followers [artist name]**
*This command will list how many followers an artist has on Spotify.*
```$followers Ariana Grande```
**$topsongs [artist name]**
*This command will list the top 10 songs for an artist on Spotify.*
```$topsongs Elvis Presley```
**$related [artist name]**
*This command will list up to 20 artists similar to an artist on Spotify.*
```$related Taylor Swift```
**$albums [artist name]**
*This commands will list an artist's top 10 albums*
```$albums Machine Gun Kelly```
